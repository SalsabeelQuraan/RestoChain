Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data privacy protected? expand_more
We use zero-knowledge proofs (ZKP) to verify that you visited a restaurant without revealing your exact coordinates or identity. Only the hash of your review is public; your personal Gmail data remains off-chain.
Where can I redeem RESTO tokens? expand_more
RESTO tokens can be redeemed directly at participating restaurants for discounts or exclusive dining experiences. Simply scan the restaurant's QR code in the app to apply your balance during checkout.
What happens if my report is invalid? expand_more
If an Oracle cross-check shows conflicting data (e.g., reporting a closed restaurant as having poor service), the report is flagged for community audit. Repeated invalid reports may lower your Trust Score.
