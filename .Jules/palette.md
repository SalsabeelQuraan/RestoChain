## 2024-12-19 - [Accessibility and Navigation in Multi-page Static Site]
**Learning:** In multi-page static sites, consistent navigation and form accessibility (labels, keyboard navigation for custom components) are often overlooked but crucial for a "pro" feel.
**Action:** Always verify that logos link back to home, form labels are correctly associated with inputs, and custom interactive elements (like dashed upload areas) are keyboard accessible and have proper ARIA roles.
