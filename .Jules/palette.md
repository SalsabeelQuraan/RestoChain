## 2025-05-15 - One Micro-Improvement Rule
**Learning:** PRs for 'Palette' must strictly adhere to the 'ONE micro-UX improvement' and 'under 50 lines' rules. Scope creep, even with good intentions, leads to rejection.
**Action:** Always identify a single, high-impact UX or accessibility win and keep the implementation extremely focused.

## 2025-05-15 - No Artifacts in Submission
**Learning:** Verification scripts and screenshots are essential for the agent's internal process but should not be committed to the repository unless they are part of the project's permanent test suite.
**Action:** Clean up all temporary verification files (scripts, videos, screenshots) before calling the submit tool.
