## 2024-05-15 - Documentation UX Navigation
**Learning:** In long documentation files, providing a Table of Contents and "Back to Top" links significantly improves scannability and navigation. However, overusing "Back to Top" links (e.g., after every small subsection) can create visual clutter. Consolidating these links to major section breaks maintains a high signal-to-noise ratio.
**Action:** Limit "Back to Top" links to major Level-2 section breaks in long Markdown documents.
