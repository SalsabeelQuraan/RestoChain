## 2025-05-15 - Form Accessibility with Explicit Labels
**Learning:** In static HTML forms, explicit 'for' and 'id' association between labels and inputs is a critical micro-UX/accessibility win. It ensures screen readers correctly announce the purpose of each field and increases the click/tap target area for users.
**Action:** Always verify that every input/textarea has a corresponding label with a matching 'for' attribute.
