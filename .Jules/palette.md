## 2024-05-23 - Micro-UX Scope and Line Limits
**Learning:** Combining multiple UX fixes (accessibility, navigation, feedback) into one PR violates the 'ONE micro-UX improvement' rule and the 50-line limit for the Palette agent. Even high-quality improvements should be atomized to ensure they are truly 'micro' and easier to review.
**Action:** Strictly adhere to the single improvement rule and line limit by focusing on the most impactful change, such as form interaction feedback.
