## 2026-06-18 - [README UX Navigation]
**Learning:** In documentation-centric repositories, a Table of Contents and strategic 'Back to Top' links significantly improve scannability. However, 'Back to Top' links should only be placed after major sections to avoid visual clutter in short documents.
**Action:** Use TOC for all documents over 3 sections; place 'Back to Top' links only after level 2 headers or long content blocks.
