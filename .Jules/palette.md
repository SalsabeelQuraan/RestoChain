## 2025-05-14 - Documentation Navigation UX
**Learning:** Over-implementing navigation aids (like "Back to Top" links) in documentation of moderate length can lead to visual clutter and disrupt the reading flow, counteracting the intended UX benefit.
**Action:** Limit "[↑ Back to Top](#table-of-contents)" links to major section breaks (Level-2 headers) to maintain a high signal-to-noise ratio in the document interface.
