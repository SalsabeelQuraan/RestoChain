## 2025-05-14 - Accessible Blockchain Interaction & Micro-UX
**Learning:** In decentralized apps with simulated on-chain latency, users benefit from both visual (spinners) and semantic (aria-busy) feedback. Custom interactive elements like bento-style dropzones and star ratings require explicit ARIA roles and keyboard listeners to be accessible. Global micro-interactions (scaling on press) should be extended to keyboard events and custom roles to maintain tactile consistency.
**Action:** Apply `aria-busy="true"` and `aria-live="polite"` to submission buttons during async states. Ensure all custom `div`-based buttons have `tabindex="0"`, `role="button"`, and handle both `click` and `keydown` (Space/Enter). Use `role="status"` for non-disruptive feedback like toast notifications.
